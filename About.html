Work, learning, and technical contributions

Semiconductor technlogy

Currently, I work as a Senior Principal Engineer at ON Semiconductor in the MOSFET Device Design group. I have been a semiconductor technologist for 15+ years, working in the field of power electronics systems, semiconductor technology development, and product innovation.

I am also leading AI/ML based projects to add value to semiconductor design and product innovations. My projects involve interesting ideas such as...

  • neural network-in-circuit-simulation loop,
  • deep learning based design quality recommendation,
  • edge AI in power management,

Formal education

I have a Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Chicago and a B.Tech in Instrumentation Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Kharagpur.

I am also pursuing an Masters of Science (MS) Degree in Analytics (specializing in Computational Data Analytics) from Georgia Tech, since Fall 2018. I plan to finish it by summer 2021.
